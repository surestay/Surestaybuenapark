NO SMOKING IN HALLWAYS OR ROOMS
Dear Guest,
It is against the law to smoke within 20 feet of a public area!
7597. (a) No public employee or member of the public shall smoke any tobacco
product inside a public building, or in an outdoor area within 20 feet of a main
exit, entrance, or operable window of a public building, or in a passenger
vehicle, as defined by Section 465 of the Vehicle Code, owned by the state.
Due to this strict regulation, smoking is not allowed in the
rooms, hallways and public areas.
The management and employees are vigilant and observant,
and should you be found smoking and discarding cigarette
butts around the property, you will be charged the smoking fee
and asked to leave the property immediately.
As a courtesy to smokers, we have provided a smoking area
on the North West corner for your indulgence.
